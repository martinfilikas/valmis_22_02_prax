Game Rules

1. Name should be 3 - 20 characters long

2. Name can contain e-mails and company names

3. Input number should be an integer number from 1-100.

4. If your guess was the same as computer’s guess, you are the winner and you will get a message: “Good job, your guess was right. The answer was ... . You have earned ... points.”

5. If your guess was not right, the program will show you a message “Sorry, your guess ... did not match with AI. Please try again.”

6. If you guessed right with first time, you will earn 3 points, if you guessed right with second time, you get 2 points and if you guessed right with third time you will get 1 point. Then after third try you will get less than 1 point. Overall you can try 50 times to guess the right answer, after that you will get a message : “Sorry, you have tried too many times, right answer was number ... “. After a third try, you will have 47 more tries left. The formula works like this: Imagine you are trying the fourth time and then you will get 1/(50-4) points.
